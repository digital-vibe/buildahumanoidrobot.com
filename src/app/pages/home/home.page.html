Welcome!

This website will focus on documenting parts, 3D printing, and building open source humanoid robots.

The goal is to get these robots in the hands of as many people as possible and for everyone to improve the designs and advance them together.

My focus in the beginning will be to document my own expereince with the InMoov robot

Feel free to contribute on GitHub!
